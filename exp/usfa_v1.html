Imagine you are a tourist and you have to navigate the subway network in an unfamiliar town.

On each trial, you will have to navigate from a starting station to a goal station, which will appear on the screen for 2 seconds:

The trial will begin with a 3..2..1.. countdown, during which you can plan your trip:

Please try to plan your trip as quickly as possible.

During your trip, you will see the name of the current station in the middle of the screen, surrounded by its neighboring stations in all four directions:

You can navigate between stations using the arrow keys (up, down, left, right). A circle means that there is no station in that direction.

Once you reach the goal station, press the space key to end the trial and start the next trial:

There will be 100 trials in the experiment. All stations are named after cities.

The experiment can only be performed on a computer. Please disable any ad-blockers before starting the experiment.
